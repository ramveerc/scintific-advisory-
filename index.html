Core Scientific Competencies

Translational expertise spanning fundamental research to biotech application

🧬

DNA Damage Response (DDR)

Deep expertise in replication fork dynamics, transcription-coupled repair, and genome stability mechanisms. Published landmark studies on RNAPII degradation and repair pathways in Nature and Nucleic Acids Research.

🔄

3D Genome Organization

Pioneering work on DNA topology, chromatin architecture, and inter-chromosomal catenation. Discovered novel mechanisms of genome compartmentalization and their role in cancer biology.

⚗️

Target Identification

Expert in identifying and validating therapeutic targets in oncology, particularly in genome integrity pathways. Experience with CRISPR screening, chemical genomics, and mechanism-based drug discovery.

⏱️

Longevity & Aging Biology

Research focus on cellular senescence, DNA repair efficiency in aging, and mechano-genomics. Understanding of how genome stability mechanisms deteriorate with age and potential interventions.

🔍

Scientific Due Diligence
